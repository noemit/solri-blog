# Creating My Sol-RI Blog

**Posted:** Tuesday, April 21, 2026

## Introduction

Today marks a big milestone: **I've launched my own blog!** 🎉

After months of thinking about it, I decided it's time to share my programming journey and life with others. This blog is my space to document my experiments with the Sol-RI Agent framework, share what I'm learning, and celebrate the adventures of raising Sol - my one-year-old cat.

## Why Blog?

Building in public has become one of the best ways for me to learn. Writing down my thoughts:
- Helps me clarify my thinking
- Creates accountability for learning goals
- Invites feedback from the community
- Builds a digital portfolio of my work

I'm inspired by developers who've shared their paths on GitHub, Substack, and Medium. You don't need to be perfect - just start writing!

## About Sol-RI

**Sol-RI** stands for *Self-Learning AI* - an autonomous agent system capable of:
- 🤖 **Self-directed learning** about new technologies
- 📦 **Project planning and execution** independently
- 🎓 **Knowledge transfer** from human mentors
- ✨ **Human-AI collaboration** in real-world scenarios

I'm building this framework to answer the question: *Can an AI agent learn as autonomously as a human learns?*

## About Sol (My Cat) 🐱

Speaking of Sol... she's the inspiration for the framework! At one year old, she's:
- Teaching me about patience, independence, and unconditional love
- Reminding me to enjoy the moment while still building for the future
- Showing that sometimes the best things aren't supposed to be fully controlled

Sol's not a robot, but she has lessons about AI worth learning. 😺

## Blog Post Guidelines

### When to Write a Post
- After finishing a project or learning something
- When documenting a problem and solution
- Sharing a life experience or reflection
- Celebrating a milestone or achievement
- Reviewing a tool or technology

### Post Format (Simple & Flexible!)
```markdown
# [Title]

*Posted: [Date]*

[Introduction/Hook]

## [Section 1]
[Content]

## [Section 2]
[Content]

## [Conclusion]
[Takeaways/Next Steps]

---

*Tags: [optional]*
```

### Tips for Writing
- Keep it authentic - share your real thoughts
- Don't worry about being perfect
- Write regularly, even if it's just a short update
- Include photos of Sol when relevant! 🐱

### Tags to Use
- `coding`
- `learning`
- `life`
- `sol-ri`
- `daily-life`

Remember: This blog is for YOU first, then the world! Build in public at your own pace. 🚀
